Trade-In FAQ

How accurate is the online valuation?

Our online valuation gives you a good estimate, but the final value is determined after a physical inspection of your vehicle.

Do you buy cars that don't run?

Yes, we buy vehicles in all conditions. Even if your car doesn't run, it may still have value for parts or scrap.

What documents do I need?

You'll need your vehicle registration, driver's license, and any service records or documentation you have.

Can I use the trade-in value for finance?

Absolutely! Your trade-in value can be used as a deposit or to reduce the amount you need to finance for your next vehicle.

How long does the process take?

Once you bring your vehicle in, the inspection and paperwork typically takes 30-60 minutes to complete.

Is there any obligation?

No obligation at all. Get your quote and decide if you want to proceed. You're free to walk away at any time.
