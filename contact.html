Frequently Asked Questions

What are your opening hours?

Our showroom is open Mon-Fri 9AM-6PM, Saturday 9AM-4PM, and Sunday 10AM-3PM. Service center hours are Mon-Fri 8AM-5PM, Saturday 8AM-2PM.

Do you offer test drives?

Yes! We encourage test drives for all our vehicles. Just bring your valid driver's license and we'll get you behind the wheel.

What finance options do you have?

We offer various finance options including traditional car loans, rent-to-own, and finance for bad credit or Centrelink recipients.

Do you provide warranties?

Yes, all our vehicles come with comprehensive warranties. The length and coverage depend on the vehicle age and condition.

Can I trade in my current vehicle?

Absolutely! We accept trade-ins on all makes and models. Get a free valuation using our online form or visit our showroom.

Do you service all makes and models?

Our service center can handle most makes and models. Contact us to confirm we can service your specific vehicle.
